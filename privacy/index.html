Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-24

the_daily is a local-first personal intelligence system with a HEALTH domain. This policy describes how data collected through the WHOOP integration is handled. The integration is a limited-scope, personal-use tool operated by an individual for their own benefit.

Information Collected

When connected to WHOOP, the_daily ingests the following personal health data from your WHOOP account:

  • Recovery score
  • Sleep performance and duration
  • Strain
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Resting heart rate
  • Respiratory rate

No other personal information is actively solicited or collected beyond what WHOOP provides through its authorized API.

How Information Is Used

WHOOP data is used exclusively to support personal health insight and daily readiness reporting inside the_daily. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside of the operator's own personal intelligence workflow.

Storage and Retention

All data is stored locally by the operator of the system — on their own machine or infrastructure. No data is sent to or stored on a central server controlled by a third party except as strictly necessary to operate the WHOOP OAuth flow itself (e.g., WHOOP's own API endpoints). WHOOP OAuth tokens may be stored locally to maintain authorized access between sessions.

Sharing

Your data is not sold. Your data is not shared with third parties for marketing, analytics, or any other purpose. The only external systems involved are WHOOP's own API infrastructure, which is required to retrieve your health data under your authorization.

OAuth and Account Access

the_daily uses WHOOP's OAuth 2.0 flow to request access to your WHOOP data on your behalf. Authorization is granted explicitly by you and is scoped to the health metrics listed above. OAuth tokens are stored locally by the operator and are used solely to retrieve your data from the WHOOP API.

Data Deletion and Revocation

You can revoke the_daily's access to your WHOOP account at any time through the WHOOP app or account settings. If you are the operator of the system and wish to delete locally stored WHOOP-derived data, you may do so by removing the relevant local data files or database entries. To request deletion of any locally stored WHOOP-derived data, contact the address below.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the integration evolves. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the date of the most recent revision. Continued use of the integration after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
